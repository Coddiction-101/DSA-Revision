# 🚀 Sorting & Searching Mastery (C++ | LeetCode)

A structured roadmap to master **Sorting and Searching techniques** using **C++** through consistent problem solving on LeetCode.

These two techniques are among the **most important patterns in coding interviews**, competitive programming, and real-world algorithm design.

This repository follows a **phase-based approach** — starting from fundamentals and gradually moving toward advanced problems.

---

# 📚 Learning Strategy

Instead of randomly solving problems, the goal is to:

1. Understand the **problem deeply**
2. Identify the **pattern (sorting / searching)**
3. Implement a **clean C++ solution**
4. Analyze **time & space complexity**
5. Recognize **patterns across problems**
